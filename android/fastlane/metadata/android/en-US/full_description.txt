You Coursify Tube turns YouTube into a focused study tool.

Paste any video or playlist link and it's saved to your personal library, organized into folders with notes. Videos play in a clean, distraction-free in-app player that shows only what you pasted — no related videos, no comments, no rabbit holes.

Features:
• Save videos and playlists into folders
• Distraction-free player — only the video you chose, nothing else
• Resume exactly where you left off, every time
• Picture-in-Picture: keep watching while you use other apps
• Landscape fullscreen with full seek control
• Take notes alongside your videos
• Sign in to YouTube for gated content
• Copy links and export / import your whole library
• Automatic on-device backups so your library is never lost

Stay on track and actually finish the courses you start.
